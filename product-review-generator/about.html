What This Tool Is

The Product Review Generator is a classroom demonstration project built for the AIML 1870 course. It shows how large language models can be prompted to produce structured, tone-controlled text from a set of inputs.

It is intended to illustrate concepts in prompt engineering, LLM API integration, and UI design. It is not a tool for creating reviews to be published anywhere.

Why This Must Not Be Used Officially
  • ● FTC Regulations (16 CFR Part 255): The Federal Trade Commission requires that all endorsements and testimonials reflect the honest opinions of genuine customers. Publishing AI-generated reviews as real customer feedback is deceptive advertising and a federal violation, subject to civil penalties.
  • ● FTC Rule on Fake Reviews (2024): In August 2024, the FTC finalized a rule explicitly banning fake or AI-generated reviews. Violations can result in civil penalties up to $51,744 per violation.
  • ● Platform Terms of Service: Amazon, Google, Yelp, Trustpilot, and virtually every major review platform prohibit fake or incentivized reviews. Submitting AI-generated reviews risks permanent account suspension, listing removal, and legal action from the platform.
  • ● Consumer Review Fairness Act (15 U.S.C. § 45b): Federal law protecting the integrity of consumer reviews. Fake reviews undermine consumer trust and may constitute unfair or deceptive acts or practices under Section 5 of the FTC Act.
  • ● State Consumer Protection Laws: Many states have their own deceptive trade practices statutes (e.g., California's UCL, New York's GBL § 349) that independently prohibit fake reviews and allow private lawsuits from harmed consumers.
  • ● Common Law Fraud: Presenting an AI-generated review as a genuine customer experience can constitute fraudulent misrepresentation, exposing individuals and businesses to civil liability.
